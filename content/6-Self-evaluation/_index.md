### Areas for Improvement (A Leader's Perspective)

While I successfully achieved our technical and project management goals, I recognize key areas where I can continue to grow to achieve higher professional standards:
*   **Optimizing Task Delegation:** During fast-paced project phases, I occasionally took on too many complex technical tasks myself rather than breaking them down and delegating them to team members. I need to improve delegation to empower my teammates and free up time for strategic oversight.
*   **Time Management Under Pressure:** Balancing deep technical research (such as optimizing token consumption for OpenAI APIs and configuring EC2 Worker instances) with managing the team's daily tasks was challenging. This sometimes resulted in personal worklogs being deferred to weekends. I plan to cultivate better discipline in daily time allocation.
*   **Deepening Cloud Architecture Knowledge:** I aim to explore advanced AWS security configurations (such as mTLS and VPC Origin, Amazon Bedrock Guardrails) and leverage Terraform to standardize our entire cloud infrastructure as code (IaC) in a more professional manner.
