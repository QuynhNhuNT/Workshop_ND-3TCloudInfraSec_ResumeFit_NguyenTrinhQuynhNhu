# Harvest Report: "Study Tour: AWS — Enterprise Cloud Architectures & Industry Applications"

### Event Information
*   **Event Name:** Study Tour: AWS — Enterprise Cloud Architectures & Industry Applications
*   **Event Date:** Saturday, July 4, 2026
*   **Location:** Bitexco Financial Tower, No. 02 Hai Trieu Street, Saigon Ward, Ho Chi Minh City
*   **Organizers:** Swinburne Vietnam in collaboration with AWS Vietnam, Renova Cloud, and Cloud Kinetics

### Event Objectives
*   **Program Goal:** To provide students with hands-on experience in Cloud Computing and Generative AI (GenAI) in enterprises, allowing them to connect and learn directly from top industry experts for career orientation.
*   **Key Messages:** Outlining the recruitment landscape in Cloud, the roadmap to transition from a student into a real Data/Cloud Engineer, how enterprises optimize data architectures and apply GenAI in the real world, and a Fireside Chat inspiring the next generation of tech talent.
*   **Value for Attendees:** Exploring the big picture of Cloud/AI markets, bridging the gap between classroom theory and real-world execution to stand out to employers, and cultivating a problem-solving mindset for business challenges.

### Speakers / Experts
*   Top experts from **AWS Vietnam**
*   Solutions Architects and Engineers from **Renova Cloud**
*   Experts from **Cloud Kinetics** and outstanding GenAI engineers

### Event Highlights
*   **Job Market & Career Trends:** The latest updates on recruitment trends in Cloud Computing globally and career opportunities for graduates.
*   **From Theory to Execution:** Real stories of converting classroom theory into enterprise-level data architectures.
*   **Fireside Chat:** An inspiring discussion on nurturing, mentoring, and developing the next generation of Vietnamese Cloud architects and developers.
*   **💡 Key Takeaway:** Technology changes every day, but a problem-solving mindset and a proactive "hands-on" attitude are the golden keys to staying ahead.

### Key Learnings
*   **Hands-on Mindset:** Be proactive in turning classroom theory into workable solutions. Focus on solving business problems rather than just learning tool features.
*   **Value of Networking:** Connecting directly with experts and speakers provides highly valuable and realistic career guidance.

### Application to My Work / Studies
*   Focus on building personal/group projects that solve real-world problems (e.g., optimizing data architecture, integrating GenAI).
*   Proactively learn and practice AWS labs to gain hands-on experience in preparation for job applications.
